GUI Help

Keyboard Interaction

Right or J
Go one frame up
Left or K
Go one frame down
Page-Up
Increase animation speed by 10%
Page-Down
Decrease animation speed by 10%
1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8 or 9
Expand object list to depth

Mouse Interaction

Left-Button-Down + Move
Rotate the entiry scene
Right-Button-Down + Move
Translate the entiry scene
Middle-Button-Down + Move
Zoom the entiry scene
D-Down + Left-Button-Down + Move
Manipulate a dragger. The dragger must be enabled using the property menu of the body before
D+Shift-Down + Left-Button-Down + Move
Manipulate a dragger in constraint motion. The dragger must be enabled using the property menu of the body before
Ctrl-Down + Left-Button-Click
Selects the body under the cursor.
Ctrl+Shift-Down + Left-Button-Click
Toggle the selection of the body under the cursor.
Ctrl-Down + Right-Button-Click
Select the body under the curser and show the property menu.
Ctrl+Shift-Down + Right-Button-Click
Toggle the selection of the body under the curser and show the property menu.
Ctrl+Alt-Down + Left-Button-Click
Shows a menu of all bodies under the cursor. Selecting one menu entry, selects this body.
Ctrl+Alt-Down + Right-Button-Click
Shows a menu of all bodies under the cursor. Selecting one menu entry, selects and shows the proptery menu of this body.
Ctrl-Down + Middle-Button-Click
Seeks the focal point of the camera to the point on the shape under the cursor.
